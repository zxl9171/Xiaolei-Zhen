Movie Eyes - HackRice

Movie Eyes reads in any movies or tv series and identifies the actors/actresses on the fly. While the movie is playing, it captures the face of the actors by using a facial recognition system, compares the face with the sample faces in our database, and then shows a list of actors'/actresses’ information right next to the video player. Movie Eyes will tell you the actor's name, profile, photo, birthday, biography, a list of other works, and his or her recent news.
